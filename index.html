About the Author

Kayla is an ESL teacher who has been writing and drawing books for fun and for various school projects since the age of 4. She is from Rochester Hills, Michigan, but has lived many places including Kiev, Ukraine, Allyn, Washington, Santa Clarita, California, and now lives in Louisville, Kentucky, with her husband Eric and her sons Leif and Patrick “Patch”. She Graduated from The Master's University in 2012 with a BA in English.
